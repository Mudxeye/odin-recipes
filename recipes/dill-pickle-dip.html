Dill Pickle Dip

The Dip, Created by God

I mean cmon, who doesnt love a good pickle dip? You can use tortilla chips, potato chips, wheat thins, etc. Really anything the heart deisres to munch down on this deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 OZ) package cream cheese, room temp
  • 1 cup chopped dill pickles, or more if you want
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped sweet onion
  • 2 tablespoons pickle juice, or even more
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Stir cream cheese in a bowl with a wooden spoon until its smooth.

  2. Stir in dill pickles, oninon, pickle juice, dill weed, salt, and pepper util distributed evenly.

  3. Refrigerate for around an hour, then serve that!
