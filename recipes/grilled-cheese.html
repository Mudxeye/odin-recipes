Grilled Cheese

Simplicity is all you need

Any 5 year old who isnt brain dead can seriously cook this. Its that simple. Yet with the right ingredients, this dish can taste like Gordon Ramsay personally came into your kitchen and shoved this splendid sandwhich straight into your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon chopped basil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill

Steps

  1. Spread 1/2 tablespoon of butter on your favorite side of each piece of bread. Put the slices of cheese on one of the slices of bread on the unbuttered side. Sprinkle parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, and dill on the other slice of bread on the unbuttered side. Put both slices together, making sure the buttered sides are facing outwards.

  2. Heat up a skillet over medium heat. When skillet is hot, lie the sandwhich in the skillet and cook each side for 3 minutes, until cheese has melted.

  3. Put that bad boy on a prison tray and enjoy!
