Chocolate Chip Cookies

The Best Chocolate Chip Cookie!

I will not waste to much of your time with over explanation for this recipes that you were looking for. What I will say is that this recipe is by far my favorite and a family favorite. Personally, I havent been able to taste a good quite as good as this recipe! Hope you enjoy :)

Ingredients

  • Parchment paper
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 OZ) bag semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. line cookies sheet with parchment paper. set aside. Add another sheet of paper, combine flour, baking soda, salt, & cinnamon.

  2. Beat together butter, brown sugar & granulated sugar in large mixing bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla.

  3. Add flour mixture gradually to butter mixture and beat until blended. stir in chocolate chips and nuts. round by tablespoons and place cookie balls onto the cookie sheet line with parchment paper.

  4. Bake 10-12 Minutes/Until lightly browned. Cool, and Enjoy!
